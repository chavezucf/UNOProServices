Frequently Asked Questions

Can I do a one time Home Cleaning Service?

Absolutely, we provide services to meet all your needs. If you only need a one time service we can do it although we definitely recommend that you at least do a monthly service.

Do I need a Home Cleaning Service to do a Repair Service?

You do not need to have a recurring House Cleaning Service ro request a Repair Service, but all of our recurring customers to receive a discount on all Repair Services.

Do you do any military discounts?

We offer a discount to any recurring customer, who is active duty milatry or a senior citizens, to both our Home Cleaning Services and Maintenance Services.

Do I need to sign a contract to get a service?

You do not need to sign a contract, but if you are selecting a recurring service you will have to fill out a service price agreement form.

Can I pay with my Credit Card?

Yes, we currently accept Cash, Master Card, Visa, and American Express as a form of payment.
